DO’S (BEST PRACTICES)
Audit your privacy settings periodically to control who can see your personal photos and posts.
Use LinkedIn to network with seniors, professors, and industry professionals in the CSE field.Fact-check
Fact-check before sharing news or links to prevent the spread of misinformation (especially AI-generated content).
Highlight your projects and technical skills (like your C++ or Python work) to build a personal brand.
Give credit to others when sharing their work, code snippets, or digital art.

DON'TS (MISTAKES TO AVOID)
Don't engage in "Cyber-arguments" or flame wars; they leave a permanent, searchable trail of negativity.
Don't post confidential info, such as your student ID, registration number, or home address.
Don't vent about professors or your university publicly; recruiters often view this as a lack of professional maturity.
Don't post while highly emotional; once a "delete" button is pressed, the screenshot may already exist.
Don't tag friends in sensitive photos without their explicit permission.
